Espumilla

Description

Although they look like ice cream that never melts. Espumillas are sold on the street and are not ice cream, but a meringue foam flavored with fruits such as guava and covered with syrup and sprinkled.


Ingredients

  • ½ cup of the pulp of the fruit from which you want the flavor of your mousse can be blackberry, guava, strawberry, naranjilla, strawberry, lemon, etc.
  • 3 egg whites
  • ½ cup of sugar

Preparation

  1. To remove the pulp, wash and clean the fruits, then cut them into small pieces and place them in a pot with level water and cook them until they are slightly soft, finally blend them and pass them through the strainer.
  2. Beat the egg whites until stiff and add the sugar to the mixture little by little while continuing to beat.
  3. Then add the fruit pulp in the same way little by little without stopping beating so that it does not lose its firm consistency.
  4. And ready you can serve it, decorate them with some sprinkles, grated coconut, blackberry sauce (arrope) or some M&M chocolates.
